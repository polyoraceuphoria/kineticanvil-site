§ 02 · HOW SANDBOX KEYS ARE SECURED

Scoped & sandbox-only

A kx_test_ key reaches the sandbox environment and synthetic data only. It cannot read, write, or even see production matters. The prefix is environment-bound.

Signed, not stored

Your key is a signed token we verify but never store. It is shown to you exactly once at creation; after that, no one, including us, can recover it.

One-time reveal

The key is returned a single time in the response that creates it. Closing this panel discards it from the browser. Lost keys are replaced, not retrieved.

Rate-limited & revocable

Each key is rate-limited and capped per organization, with a self-serve revoke + rotate path. Abuse controls and inactivity expiry keep stale keys from lingering.
