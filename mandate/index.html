§ PRODUCT· MANDATE

A dedicated operating room for a single judgment.

Anvil powers your Mandate. Turn a matter into a Mandate.

Most judgments stall because the work of collecting them lives in inboxes, spreadsheets, and memory. A Mandate is a productized operational instance scoped to one matter: provisioned in minutes, run on the Anvil protocol, branded to your firm, and sealed into an exportable record at close. One judgment. One room. Worked to resolution.

§ 01 · THE INSTANCE

Not a seat. A matter.

You do not buy software by the user. You stand up a Mandate for the judgment you are collecting. It opens with the order as its root authority, turns on the stations the matter needs, and stays live for the full lifecycle: intake, trace, enforcement, recovery, distribution, close.

A Mandate is a state, not a document. It moves from open to active to recovered to closed, and the Anvil API lays out the action steps at every stage so the next move is never a guess. Run one, or run a book of mandates side by side.

It feels bespoke because it is scoped to your matter and branded to your firm. It is not custom-built. Every Mandate runs the same protocol and the same tamper-evident record underneath, which is what lets it stand up in minutes and hold up in court.

§ 02 · TWO FACES

One instance. Two views.

Operator Console

Where you work the matter

Your team's working surface. The ten Anvil stations, scoped to this judgment, with the asset graph, the recovery ledger, and document generation in one place.

  • Asset graph and attribution, each edge source-linked
  • Append-only recovery ledger, recovered-to-date live
  • Enforcement package: freeze, garnishment, turnover
  • Exportable, court-formatted audit record
Client View

What your client sees

An attorney-branded progress page for the creditor you represent. You control what is published. They see momentum, not a black box, and you look like the institution handling it.

  • Your firm's name, mark, and accent on the page
  • Milestone timeline and recovered-to-date
  • Published updates you write and release on your schedule
  • Read-only, secure, on your own subdomain

§ 04 · THE CLIENT WINS YOU REPEAT WORK

Progress your client can watch.

Recovery is slow, and silence reads as inaction. The Client View turns the matter into something your creditor can follow: a branded page that shows where their recovery stands, what just moved, and what comes next. You decide what is published and when. The trail behind it is the same record that holds up on the stand, so nothing you show is something you cannot defend.

That is the difference between a vendor and the firm a client refers. The Mandate is built to earn the next matter.

§ 06 · LIFECYCLE

Open it, work it, seal it.

A Mandate lives as long as the matter does. When the judgment is satisfied or the matter closes, the full record is sealed and handed to you: a tamper-evident, exportable archive of the trace, the actions, and the recovery. Park it at a reduced rate or close it out. The evidence stays yours.
