§ 02 · TRACE

BTC and ETH fund-flow tracing

crypto.trace follows outflows across bounded hops and returns a structured map for review.

INPUT

Wallets, chain, and hop limit associated with a matter.

BTC

Outflows followed hop by hop.

ETH

Native ETH and ERC-20 outflows followed across bounded hops.

OUTPUT

Wallets, flows, terminal nodes, and confidence-labeled hypotheses.

BOUNDARY

Hypotheses require review. They do not establish custody, control, authority, or recovery.
