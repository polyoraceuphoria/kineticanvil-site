§ 04 · MANDATE

Turn a matter into a Mandate

A Mandate is a dedicated operating room for a single matter: branded to your firm, live for the lifecycle of the judgment. It is a state, not a document, and the Anvil API lays out the action steps at every stage. Your team works it on the protocol; your client follows a branded progress page you control. Run one, or a book of mandates. Priced per matter, not per seat.

§ 05 · GOVERNANCE

Built for regulated recovery

Every operation is authority-gated, fully audited, and source-linked on an immutable trail. Sanctions and bankruptcy gates clear where applicable. Kinetic Anvil holds no client funds. The platform builds the case and the record; people with the authority make the moves.
