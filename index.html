§ 01 · CURRENT CONTRACT

Contract facts

REST resources hold persisted protocol state. Jobs dispatch capabilities through a single asynchronous contract. Of the registered job verbs, only crypto.trace accepts work; other verb types refuse with an error.

LIVE VERB
crypto.trace
Submit through POST /v1/jobs. The response is a queued Job that can be retrieved by job ID.
RESOURCE PLANE
Persisted records
Versioned REST resources expose protocol records under the dated contract.
JOB PLANE
Asynchronous work
Capabilities are job types with queued, running, succeeded, failed, or canceled status.
VERSION PIN
Dated contract
